Suramar

The ancient city of the Nightborne elves, shrouded in mystery and protected by a powerful magical shield for ten thousand years

The Tale of Suramar

A city frozen in time, its people transformed, its fate intertwined with the Legion

The Sundering

When the Well of Eternity collapsed during the War of the Ancients, Grand Magistrix Elisande erected a mighty shield to protect Suramar City. This barrier saved the city but isolated it from the rest of the world for 10,000 years.

The Nightwell

To sustain themselves, the Suramar elves created the Nightwell from the Eye of Aman'Thul. Over millennia, their dependence on its arcane energy transformed them into the Nightborne, a new race forever changed by their isolation.

History

Suramar was the location of the Temple of Elune, the central place of worship for the night elves. The high priestess of Elune lived in the temple. The Boughs of Azshara, Izal-Shurah and Arauk-Nashal were also located here.
